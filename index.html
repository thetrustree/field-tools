Getting Started
How to use Field Tools with your technicians
⚡ Step 1 — Set Up Your Partners
Go to Financing → Partner Setup. Your default lenders (Service Finance, Wells Fargo, Synchrony) are already loaded. You can:
• Toggle Visible on/off for individual plans
• Click Hide on a partner to remove them from Field View without deleting them
• Add custom partners and plans for your market
• Use Copy Team Link to share your setup with your whole team instantly
🔢 Step 2 — Enter Job Amounts in Field View
Go to Financing → Field View. Tap a colored job slot and type the job amount. Each slot gets a color (green, yellow, red, purple…). You can run up to 6 jobs side by side.

Tap a slot to see all eligible financing plans sorted by lowest monthly payment. The Lowest Payment and Lowest Interest cards appear at the top so you can lead with the best option at the table.
🏦 Step 3 — Use the Loan Guide for FICO Conversations
Switch to the Loan Guide tab. Set the customer's FICO score with the slider — you'll instantly see:
• Estimated APR range and monthly payment
• Which lenders are accessible at that score
• Full FICO tier table for comparison shopping
• Lender profiles with strengths and watch-outs

Use this to set realistic expectations before the customer applies.
📋 Office View — Dealer Fee Summary
Switch to Financing → Office View to see a cost-to-company breakdown across all jobs. This shows dealer fee impact by plan so the office knows the real margin on each financed job.
💡 Pro Tips
• Copy Field Link in Partner Setup sends techs a read-only version locked to Field View only — they can't edit plans
• Plans with a 0 pay factor are SAC (Same As Cash) — payment = amount ÷ term
• Service Finance goes up to 180 months (15 years) — great for large installs
• GreenSky prefers 650+ FICO; Wells Fargo is most trusted with homeowners but slowest to fund
• Bookmark this page on your phone home screen for one-tap access in the field
